Why do you have to vaccinate?

There are three important reasons why you should vaccinate which we describe as follows: Build immunity againts viruses, build hard immunity and Lower levels of virus exposure.

Build hard immunity

This immunity provides robust and sustained protection against future encounters with the same pathogen, reducing the risk of illness or infection. Usually through vaccination.

Build immunity againts viruses

This is typically achieved through vaccinations, a healthy lifestyle, and a balanced diet, which can enhance the immune system's ability to fight off viral invaders.

Lower levels of virus exposure

This reduced exposure can lead to a lower risk of infection or a milder illness if infected. Measures like social distancing and mask-wearing aim to reduce virus exposure.
